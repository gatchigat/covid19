Info COVID-19

The continuously increasing number of COVID-19 cases in the world The government implemented a total lockdown that lasted for several months. During those days, no one is allowed to leave their home. This lockdown helps to avoid the spread of deadly diseases. Also, people started to follow the health protocols that the government provided.

This lockdown limits people's movement, from getting out of their house, going to groceries and other situations that will make people exposed to others. They became less interactive, losing face to face contact with people can be stressful, a feeling of worry, frightened and others that is unbearable. Not getting enough contact can leave us feeling lonely and alone with our problems. Which can have a great impact in people's mental health, as this pandemic continues it may cost a lot of risk from people. Specially relationship with our love ones, stay in touch or connected can be done through phone calls, emails, messages etc. But with them beside you gives a different kind of feeling, thru technology we don't feel confident or comfortable. We're losing closure, bonds, making a weaker relationship to people and sometimes because of less interaction we ended up isolating ourselves to others.
